RECOMMENDED CONSTRAINTS
======================

Based on Phase 1.5 analysis, implement these constraints:

1. Minimum Hard Cap: 100,000 tokens
2. Maximum Initial Reward: 10% of (Hard Cap - TGE)
3. Minimum Initial Valuation: $1,000 (burn_unit >= 200,000)
4. Minimum Epochs: 3
5. Maximum First Epoch Mint: 30% of remaining supply

Validation formula:
const maxReward = (hardCap - tgeAllocation) * 0.1;
